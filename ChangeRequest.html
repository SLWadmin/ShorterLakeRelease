Exterior Change Requirements

To insure Condominium and Association bylaws and covenents are followed, all
Co-Owners wishing to add or modify the exterior of their property should
obtain approval from assocation officers and or the architectural committee.


A partial list of exterior improvements/ modifications requiring approval are:

  • Home Addition
  • Adding or Modifying a Shed
  • Adding or Modifying a fence
  • Removal of Established live trees
  • Storing Equipment in a Common Area

Before acquiring a building a permit, association approval is required
