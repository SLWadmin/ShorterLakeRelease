Shorter Lake Woods Condominiums Association

Bylaws and Covenants to Govern and Manage the Condominium Project

Condominium Bylaws
Condominium Bylaws were created by the developer of the Shorter Lake Woods Condominium Project. They were
regestered in Benzi County allong with the "Master Deed" of the Condominium Project. These Bylaws provide
the framwork for the creation of the Shorter Lake Woods Co-Owner Bylaws, described below. If a conflict
should arrise between these two sets of Bylaws, the Condominium Bylaws takes precedence over the Association
Bylaws                                                 Condominium Bylaws


Association Bylaws
Shorter Lake Woods Condominium Association has been incorporated under the laws of the State of Michigan as
a nonprofit corporation for Governing and Managing Condominiums.    Association Bylaws were adopted as prescribed in
in the Condominium Bylaws.    These Bylaws and Covenants are followed and enforced by the Association's officers.
Approval and signoff by Association officers are required to obtain a waiver of an Association Covenant.

A Building or Zoning permit from Almira Township does not constitute a Covenant Waiver.    Both the Condominium
and Association Bylaws take precedence over Almira permits.

There is no Statute of Limitation on Covenant Violations.     Action to enforce a non waived Covenant violation can
be taken at any time by a Co-Owner or the Association.
